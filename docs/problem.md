# Business Problem

Enterprise business operations across SAP S/4HANA, email systems, and knowledge platforms are fragmented and require manual coordination.

Users must navigate multiple systems to retrieve data, trigger actions, and complete workflows, leading to delays, inefficiencies, and errors.

Existing automation solutions are rule-based and lack the ability to dynamically understand user intent, make decisions, and execute cross-system actions intelligently.

There is a need for an AI-driven system that can understand natural language requests and autonomously orchestrate actions across SAP and enterprise systems.
